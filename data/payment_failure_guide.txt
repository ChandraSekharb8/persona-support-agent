Payment Failure Troubleshooting Guide

Overview
Payment failures occur when the subscription payment cannot be completed. The issue may be caused by card details, bank restrictions, billing address mismatch, payment gateway timeout, or insufficient balance.

Common Payment Failure Reasons
- Expired debit or credit card
- Incorrect card number
- Incorrect CVV
- Insufficient balance
- Bank declined the transaction
- International payments disabled
- Billing address mismatch
- Payment gateway timeout
- Card spending limit exceeded

Customer Resolution Steps
Ask the customer to:
1. Confirm that the card details are correct.
2. Check whether the card is expired.
3. Verify the billing address.
4. Confirm that sufficient balance is available.
5. Check whether online and international payments are enabled.
6. Retry the payment after 10 minutes.
7. Use a different payment method if the issue continues.

Updating Payment Method
Customers can update payment information from:

Account Settings -> Billing -> Payment Method -> Update

After updating the payment method, the customer can retry the failed invoice payment from the Billing page.

Failed Renewal Payment
If auto-renewal payment fails:
- The customer may receive a payment failure email.
- The system may retry payment automatically.
- The account may enter a grace period.
- Some premium features may be restricted after the grace period.

Payment Gateway Timeout
If the payment page times out:
1. Ask the customer not to refresh repeatedly.
2. Ask them to wait 10 minutes.
3. Check whether the invoice shows paid or unpaid.
4. If unpaid, retry payment.
5. If paid but subscription is not updated, escalate to billing support.

Pending Bank Authorization
Sometimes banks show a temporary pending charge. Pending authorization usually disappears within 24–48 hours if the payment was not completed.

Escalation Conditions
Escalate to human support if:
- Payment was deducted but invoice is unpaid
- Duplicate payment is visible
- Customer paid but plan was not activated
- Bank confirms successful payment but dashboard shows failed
- Customer is an enterprise account
- Billing contact or tax invoice correction is required
