# Refund Policy Guide

## Overview
Refund requests are reviewed by the billing support team. The AI assistant should never guarantee or approve a refund automatically.

## Refund Review
Refund eligibility depends on:
- Subscription type
- Billing date
- Usage after payment
- Contract terms
- Duplicate payment evidence
- Account history
- Local tax and compliance rules

## Common Refund Request Scenarios
Customers may request refunds for:
- Duplicate charges
- Accidental renewal
- Wrong plan selection
- Service outage impact
- Cancellation after renewal
- Unauthorized payment
- Unused subscription period

## Refund Request Steps
Ask the customer to provide:
1. Account email
2. Invoice number
3. Payment date
4. Payment amount
5. Reason for refund request
6. Screenshot or bank reference if duplicate charge is claimed

## Duplicate Charge Refund
If the customer reports duplicate charges:
1. Ask whether both transactions are settled or one is pending.
2. Explain that pending bank authorization may disappear within 24–48 hours.
3. If both charges are settled, escalate to billing support.
4. Include payment IDs and invoice details in the handoff summary.

## Unauthorized Payment
Unauthorized payment is sensitive. Do not attempt to resolve automatically.

Immediate steps:
1. Ask the customer to secure their account.
2. Ask them to change password.
3. Ask them to review active sessions.
4. Escalate to human billing and security support.

## Refund Approval
Only the billing team can approve or reject refunds. The assistant may explain the process but must not promise the outcome.

## Escalation Conditions
Escalate to human support if:
- Customer demands immediate refund
- Duplicate charges are confirmed
- Unauthorized payment is reported
- Legal threat is mentioned
- Tax invoice dispute is raised
- Enterprise contract refund is requested
