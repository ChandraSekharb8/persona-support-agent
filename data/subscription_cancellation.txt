Subscription Cancellation Guide

Overview
Customers can cancel their subscription from the Billing section. Cancellation prevents future renewal charges but does not always delete the account immediately.

Cancellation Location
Customers can cancel from:

Account Settings -> Billing -> Manage Subscription -> Cancel Plan

Cancellation Steps
1. Log in to the account.
2. Open Account Settings.
3. Select Billing.
4. Click Manage Subscription.
5. Select Cancel Plan.
6. Confirm the cancellation reason.
7. Submit cancellation request.

Effect of Cancellation
After cancellation:
- The subscription will not renew in the next billing cycle.
- Paid features remain active until the end of the current billing period.
- Workspace data remains available according to the data retention policy.
- Users may reactivate before the billing cycle ends.

Downgrade Instead of Cancel
If the customer wants to reduce cost, they may downgrade to a lower plan instead of cancellation.

Downgrade path:

Account Settings -> Billing -> Change Plan -> Select Lower Plan

Data Retention
After cancellation, workspace data may be retained for a limited period. Customers should export important reports, invoices, and files before the retention period ends.

Reactivation
Customers can reactivate from:

Account Settings -> Billing -> Reactivate Plan

If the plan has already expired, the customer may need to choose a new subscription plan.

Refunds
Cancellation does not automatically create a refund. Refund eligibility must be reviewed by the billing team.

Escalation Conditions
Escalate to human support if:
- Customer requests refund during cancellation
- Customer cannot access Billing page
- Enterprise contract cancellation is requested
- Customer wants immediate data deletion
- Account owner is unavailable
- Legal, compliance, or tax concerns are mentioned
